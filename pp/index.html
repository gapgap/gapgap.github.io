Support

Need help with What is It?

This page is for users who need help, want to report a problem, or have questions about the What is It? app.

If you contact us, please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue so we can help faster.

Email Support How To Get Help
Support email: gapgapus@gmail.com

We aim to respond to support requests as soon as possible.

How to get help

  • Email us with your question or support request.
  • Describe what happened and the steps that led to the issue.
  • Attach screenshots if they help explain the problem.
  • Mention that your request is about What is It?.

What we can help with

  • Bug reports and unexpected app behavior.
  • Questions about app features and how to use them.
  • Account or access issues, where applicable.
  • General feedback and improvement suggestions.

Helpful details to include

  • Device model and iOS version.
  • App version number.
  • Time and date when the issue happened.
  • Any error message you saw on screen.

Privacy questions

If your question is about privacy or data handling, please email us at gapgapus@gmail.com and mention that your request is privacy-related.
